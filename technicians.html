TECHCELLENCE PROGRAM

Students accepted into the #1 Cochran TechEx Center at Rosedale for automotive or collision repair training also have the option to apply for the #1 Cochran TECHcellence Program.

Through this program, students can earn a part-time paycheck while receiving valuable training from #1 Cochran expert technicians. Students can also request the #1 Cochran facility where they would like to be mentored.

Working alongside experienced Certified Master Technicians and under the supervision of a seasoned Manager, you’ll learn the key skills necessary to succeed as a Service Technician or Collision Repair Technician.

Special benefits include:

  • Tool allowance
  • Student loan assistance
  • Relocation assistance
