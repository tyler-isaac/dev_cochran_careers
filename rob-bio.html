GET TO KNOW ROB COCHRAN

CHAIRMAN & CEO, #1 COCHRAN

Rob Cochran sits atop the 39th largest automotive dealer group in the nation. But when it comes to running the enterprise that bears his name, there is little sitting involved. As #1 Cochran’s Chairman and Chief Executive, Rob directs all facets of the organization’s operations. His presence is ubiquitous. From boardrooms to showrooms, in communities and on TV, Rob steers the company with innovative leadership, vision and spirit.

A second-generation owner, Rob joined the family business as Executive Vice President in 1987 after graduating from Carnegie Mellon University. In 1991, as Rob’s father and company founder, Bob Cochran, battled an illness, Rob became President and CEO at just 25 years of age. Two years later, Mr. Cochran passed away.

In the years since, Rob has carried the company to new heights and served as the catalyst for its tremendous growth. But of all his achievements, Rob is most proud that a third generation of Cochrans has entered the business, with some of his adult children taking roles with the company. Just like its past and present, #1 Cochran’s future is family-owned.

On the essence of his approach as CEO, he says:

“OUR LEADERSHIP IS EMPOWERED. I THINK THAT’S ONE OF THE REASONS WE’VE BEEN FORTUNATE ENOUGH TO ATTRACT AND RETAIN HIGHER-QUALITY PEOPLE. THEY ARE GIVEN A LOT OF LATITUDE WHEN NECESSARY. THE MOST RELATIONSHIP-MINDED COMPANIES ARE GOING TO WIN.”
